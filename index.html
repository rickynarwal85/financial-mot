Who Is This For?

You don't need to be in financial trouble to benefit from a Financial MOT. Most people just want clarity.

The "Where Does It All Go?" Person

You earn a decent salary but somehow never have as much left over as you'd expect. A Financial MOT shows you exactly where it goes.

Couples Managing Joint Finances

When two people's spending habits combine, blind spots appear. A neutral, expert review helps you both see the full picture without the awkward conversation.

About to Make a Big Decision

Buying a house, changing jobs, starting a family — before any major financial step, it helps to know exactly where you stand today.

Wanting to Save More, Not Sure How

You know you should be saving more but cutting back feels overwhelming. A Financial MOT shows you where the low-effort wins actually are.

Your Data is Safe

Sharing your bank statements takes trust. Here's exactly how your information is handled.

Encrypted Transfer

Statements are sent via WhatsApp or email — both of which use end-to-end or transport encryption. You choose whichever you're most comfortable with.

No Third-Party Sharing

Your statements and financial information are never shared with any third party — not partners, not advertisers, not anyone. Ever.

Permanently Deleted Within 14 Days

Once your review is delivered, all copies of your statements are permanently deleted within 14 days. Nothing is retained beyond what's needed to produce your summary.

Confidential Review Process

Your review is produced solely by Ricky and seen by no one else. It's a private, one-to-one service — your finances are nobody else's business.
